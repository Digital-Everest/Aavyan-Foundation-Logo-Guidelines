06 — Misuse

What Not To Do

Consistent application of the logo builds recognition and trust. The following alterations are strictly prohibited.

Don't Distort or Stretch
Never alter the logo's proportions. Use constrained scaling only.
Don't Recolor the Logo
Only approved color combinations may be used. No custom tints or gradients.
Don't Rotate
The logo must always appear upright. No rotations at any angle.
Don't Use Busy Backgrounds
Logo must appear on solid, approved background colors only.
Drop shadow!
Don't Add Effects
No drop shadows, glows, outlines, or filters should be applied.
Aavyan
Foundation
Don't Change the Typeface
The wordmark must never be typeset in any font other than the approved brand font.
